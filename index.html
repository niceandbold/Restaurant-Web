About Tavern


since 2012

The Tavern is the first outlet of our group with a gastro pub concept. The Tavern logo is a four leaf clover that symbolizes the holy trinity of Hope, Faith, Love and Good Luck. It is a place to chill out after a tiring day at work and to indulge you in our delicious grills and refreshing beers. Either a get togehter moment with family, friends, colleagues or just hanging out and watch the latest sports game. The interior itself reflex a mixture old yet modern industrial theme. Casual conversations, good laugh, great foods, and a friendly service.
